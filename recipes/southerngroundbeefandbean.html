Southern Ground Beef and Bean Skillet

One pan ground beef and bean, southern style

Easy ground beef one-skillet dinner for a busy weeknight. Tasty, savory, and great with cornbread or home fries (frozen French fries work too).

Ingredients

  • 1 Pound Ground Beef

  • 1 Cup Diced Onion

  • 1 Cup Diced Green Bell Pepper

  • 2 Cloves Garlic, Minced

  • 1/4 Cup Medium Salsa

  • 1/2 TBSP Greek Seasoning

  • 1/2 tsp Ground Black Pepper

  • Kosher Salt to Taste

  • 1 (15 oz) Can Southwestern-Style Beans, Undrained

Steps

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir beef in the hot skillet until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes. Add onion and bell pepper and cook until onion is softened and translucent, about 5 minutes. Add garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.

  2. Add in salsa, Greek seasoning, pepper, and salt. Cook and stir until well combined, 2 to 3 minutes. Stir in beans. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, and simmer to desired thickness, about 20 minutes. Add water if sauce becomes too thick.
