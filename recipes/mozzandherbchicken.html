Crunchy Panko-Coated Chicken with Couscous and Roasted Carrots

Not much else beats crunchy panko-coated chicken. Savory, buttery couscous and tender roasted carrots. The best part? Everything comes together in just 30 minutes! Talk about a weeknight win.

Ingredients

  • 24 oz Carrots
  • 1 Lemon
  • 1/2 Cup Panko Breadcrumbs (Contains Wheat)
  • 1 Cup Mozzarella Cheese (Contains Milk)
  • 1 TBSP Italian Seasoning
  • 1 tsp Chili Flakes
  • 20 oz Chicken Cutlets
  • 4 TBSP Sour Cream (Contains Milk)
  • 5 oz Israeli Couscous (Contains Wheat)
  • 2 Packets Chicken Stock Concentrate

Steps

  1. Adjust rack to top and middle positions and preheat oven to 425 degrees. Wash and dry produce.
  2. Trim, peel, and cut carrots on a diagonal into 1/2-inch-think pieces. Zest and quarter lemon
  3. In a medium bowl, combine panko, mozzarella, 2 tsp Italian Seasoning, 2 TBSP olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  4. Toss carrots on baking sheet with a drizzle of olive oil, salt, pepper, and a pinch of chili flakes to taste.
  5. Roast on top rack for 15-20 minutes (or until browned and tender).
  6. Pat chicken dry with paper towels; season all over with salt and pepper.
  7. Evenly spread sour cream onto tops of chicken, then mound with panko mixture, pressing firmly to adhere (no need to coat the undersides).
  8. Roast on top rack until chicken is golden brown and cooked through and carrots are browned and tender, 15-20 minutes.
  9. While carrots and chicken roast, melt 2 TBSP butter in a small pot over medium-high heat. Add couscous and a pinch of salt. Cook, stirring, until toasted, 2-3 minutes.
  10. Add 1 1/2 cup water and stock concentrate. Bring to a boil, then cover and reduce to a low simmer. Cook until couscous is tender, 6-8 minutes.
  11. Drain any excess liquid if necessary.
  12. Once chicken is done, stir 2 TBSP butter into couscous until melted. Stir in lemon zest and lemon juice to taste; season with salt and pepper.
  13. Divide couscous, chicken, and carrots between plates. Serve with any remaining lemon wedges on the side.
